ReplyMate Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

Introduction

ReplyMate is a Chrome extension that helps Gmail users generate AI-powered email replies. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Anonymous User ID: A unique identifier generated by your browser using cryptographic methods (UUID). This ID is stored locally in your browser and helps us track usage and manage your subscription.
  • Usage Data: Number of AI replies you generate and your current subscription plan (Free, Pro, Pro+).
  • Subscription Information: Stripe customer ID and subscription ID for billing purposes (only if you upgrade to a paid plan).
  • Technical Data: Browser type, extension version, and error logs that do not contain personal information.

Information You Provide

  • Email Context: Email subject, message content, sender/recipient names, and conversation thread. This data is processed temporarily for AI reply generation and is not stored in our database.
  • User Preferences: Reply tone (Professional, Friendly, Polite), length preferences, and language settings.
  • Optional Information: Your name for signature generation and email address for payment processing (optional).

How We Use Information

  • Service Delivery: To generate AI email replies and provide them to you in real-time.
  • Usage Management: To track your reply count against your plan limits and manage billing cycles.
  • Customer Support: To assist you with technical issues and account management.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve ReplyMate's functionality.
  • Billing Processing: To manage your subscription through Stripe's secure payment system.

Third-Party Services

OpenAI

We use OpenAI's API to generate email replies. When you request a reply:

  • Your email context is sent to OpenAI's servers
  • OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy
  • We do not share your data with OpenAI beyond what's necessary for reply generation
  • Email content is not stored by OpenAI long-term

OpenAI Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

Stripe

We use Stripe for secure payment processing:

  • Payment information is handled directly by Stripe's secure systems
  • We only store Stripe customer ID and subscription ID in our database
  • Credit card details are never stored on our servers
  • All payment processing complies with PCI DSS standards

Stripe Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

Data Storage and Retention

Our Database

We maintain a secure SQLite database that stores:

  • Anonymous User ID: Your unique identifier (no personal information)
  • Plan Information: Current subscription plan and billing cycle dates
  • Usage Count: Number of replies generated in current billing cycle
  • Stripe IDs: Customer and subscription identifiers (only for paid plans)
  • Timestamps: Account creation, last update, and billing cycle dates

Email Content

Email content is never stored in our database. We only process it temporarily:

  • Sent to OpenAI for reply generation
  • Deleted from our servers after the AI response is generated
  • Not used for any other purpose
  • Not accessible to our team after processing

Local Storage

The extension stores some data locally in your browser:

  • User Preferences: Language, tone, and length settings
  • Usage Cache: Temporary usage data (expires after 30 seconds)
  • User ID: Your anonymous identifier

Data Retention Period

  • User Account Data: Retained indefinitely unless you request deletion
  • Usage History: Current billing cycle data only
  • Email Content: Not retained (temporary processing only)
  • Local Cache: Automatically expires after 30 seconds

Security

We implement multiple security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All communications between your browser and our servers are encrypted
  • Anonymous Identification: No personal identifiers collected, only cryptographic UUIDs
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect data essential for service delivery
  • Secure Third-Party Services: Use of reputable services (OpenAI, Stripe) with strong security practices
  • Regular Security Updates: Our systems are regularly updated and monitored
  • Access Controls: Database access is strictly controlled and logged

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all data we have about you through your extension settings
  • Correction: Update your user preferences and account information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Restriction: Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Complaint: File a complaint with data protection authorities if needed

Children's Privacy

ReplyMate is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will immediately delete this information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will:

  • Post any changes on this page
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Notify users of significant changes through the extension

Your continued use of ReplyMate after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
